Why does your business need online presence?

Not so long ago, Facebook was a place where one went to post a bouquet of flowers on ones “wall” and say “Hello!”. It was the centre of interaction for friends living close and friends living far. Now, Facebook (one amongst hundreds of platforms) is actively used as a marketing platform for brand promotion. Where could be a better place to promote your brand other than where your market goes to cool off? “A business does not exist if it is not online” – when a potential consumer seeks out a new service or product, typically, their first instinct would be to “Google it”. If your brand / product does not appear when googled (yes, ‘googled’ is now a word in the oxford dictionary) … then your brand is in need of a little Social Spice.

Who we are & what we do:
